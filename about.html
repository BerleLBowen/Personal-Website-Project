Who is Berle Bowen

Berle’s Bio: For over 7+ years, Berle Bowen has been a noteworthy leader in the finance realm. He is a member of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. In addition, Berle is an entrepreneur and has his own skincare line Safi-Skin Care LLC, but Berle can also be credited with contributions to diversity, equity, and inclusion. Berle has been awarded with an honorable discharge from the Army National Guard. He holds a Master’s Degree in Organizational Leadership from Colorado State University and currently resides in Englewood, Colorado. In his personal time, Berle likes to workout, listen to music, and hanging with friends.
